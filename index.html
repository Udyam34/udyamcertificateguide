How to Download Your Udyam Registration Certificate Online (2026 Guide)

The Udyam Registration Certificate is the official proof that your business is registered as a Micro, Small, or Medium Enterprise (MSME) in India. You need it to claim MSME benefits — priority and collateral-free lending under CGTMSE, protection against delayed payments, subsidies, and preference in government tenders. This guide walks through how to download and print it online, what details you need, and what to do if you have misplaced your Udyam number.

What you need before you start

  • Your Udyam Registration Number (URN) — it looks like UDYAM-XX-00-0000000.
  • Access to the mobile number or email you used during registration (for OTP verification).

If you have not registered yet, you will first need to register your business under Udyam before a certificate can be issued.

Step-by-step: download your certificate

  1. Open the official Udyam portal and go to the "Print / Verify" menu.
  2. Select "Print Udyam Certificate."
  3. Enter your Udyam Registration Number and your registered mobile number or email.
  4. Choose how to receive the OTP (registered mobile or email) and click Validate & Generate OTP.
  5. Enter the OTP and submit. Your certificate opens as a PDF that you can download or print.
If the OTP does not arrive, wait about a minute and request it again, and confirm you are using the mobile number linked to your registration. OTP delays are almost always network-related, not an account problem.

Lost or forgotten your Udyam number?

You cannot download the certificate without the URN — but you can recover it. The portal lets you retrieve a forgotten Udyam number using your registered mobile and an OTP; once you have the number back, return to the steps above. If you would rather have the verification and printing handled for you, you can also download your Udyam certificate through a guided service.

Frequently asked questions

Is the Udyam certificate free? Yes — registration and the certificate are issued free of cost on the official government portal. Some people use guided services for convenience, but there is no government fee.

Does the certificate expire? No. The Udyam certificate is lifetime-valid and carries a dynamic QR code; you do not need to renew it, though you should keep your business details updated.

Can I download it without the URN? No — recover your Udyam number first, then follow the download steps above.

This is an informational guide about the MSME / Udyam registration process in India. For official registration, always refer to the Government of India's Udyam portal.
